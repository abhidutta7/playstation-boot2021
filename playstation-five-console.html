PlayStation 5 Console

The PlayStation 5 offers a fast loading speed, and ultra-high SSD, and haptic feedback. When you buy the PS5, in the box comes a console, a DualSense wireless controller, and a USB Type C to Type A charging cable. The PS5 has a much bigger storage capacity than its predecessor and the actual specs of the console are much better.

PS5

Purchsing the PS5 allows you to play cross-generation. This means you can play with your friends who still have a PS4. Moreover, there will be cross-platform playing which means that PlayStation players and Xbox players will be able to play together and have more fun.
